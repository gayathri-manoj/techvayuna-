About Us

TechVayuna is a student-led initiative fostering innovation, teamwork, and leadership. We inspire students to explore, create, and excel in technology through events, workshops, and hackathons. TechVayuna is one of the finest technical clubs of SRM IST, Ramapuram. Over the years, TechVayuna has successfully organized flagship events, providing students with valuable exposure to emerging technologies and opportunities to showcase their talent
